Mud and Water Kitchen

Get messy, experiment, and cook up muddy masterpieces! Little Farmers love splashing, scooping, and stirring their way through this sensory-rich outdoor play zone. Bare feet welcome.

Orchard and Gardens

Each child will have their own raised garden bed! Little Farmers can grow their own snack — tomatoes, watermelon, or celery for ants-on-a-log — and are encouraged to plant something they don’t like. With patience and pride, broccoli, beets, and brussels sprouts become badges of bravery and accomplishment. From dirt to plate, our food is part of our play, our learning, and our joy. A garden teaches patience. A harvest teaches gratitude.

Backyard

This is where community gathers. Chicks scratch the ground near bare toes, imagination blooms under the trees, and quiet moments are shared with nature’s smallest friends.

Eagles Nest

Welcome to the treetop sanctuary! The Eagle’s Nest is a cozy corner of the orchard where Little Farmers can create, pretend, relax, and build using foraged sticks, twigs, and leaves. It’s imagination in motion.
