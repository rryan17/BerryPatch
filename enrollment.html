Tuition

$320/month per child

This breaks down to $80 per week for two mornings filled with farm care, garden play, nature crafts, animal visits, and joyful discovery.

Payment Information

At The Berry Patch, we like to keep things simple and personal. Tuition is collected monthly, and we kindly accept cash or check only.

This helps us avoid pesky processing fees — making sure more of your contribution goes directly toward nourishing the garden, caring for the animals, and growing joyful experiences for your child. Your tuition will not change while your child is enrolled.

Enrollment Fee

A one-time $75 enrollment fee secures your child's spot in the program and helps us prepare all the magical materials they'll need — from garden tools and seeds to muddy boots and berry baskets.

How to Enroll

  1. Complete the Registration Form
  2. Schedule a visit with Miss Rachael
  3. Pay deposit
  4. Get ready for a season of mud pies, chicken cuddles, garden magic, and memories that last!
