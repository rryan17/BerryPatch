Who we are

Welcome to Berry Patch Farm School, a home-based childcare program rooted in nature, wonder, and connection. We believe in the magic of childhood — muddy boots, curious questions, and caring hearts. Here at Berry Patch, we center our days around plants, animals, growth, good food, and even better company.

We strive to slow down — to give children the space to take their time, notice the little things, and savor each season. Our projects are often long and layered — from seed to root to harvest, from scrap wood to treehouse. We believe that when kids are given time and trust, they build patience, pride, and deep understanding.

Our philosophy is simple: follow the joy. If something sparks curiosity or lights up a smile — we follow that path. Through playful exploration and meaningful moments on our farm, we aim to nurture a love of the natural world, inspire wonder, and foster empathy in every child.

Let them be little. Let them be wild. Let them be.
