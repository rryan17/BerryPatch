The Orchard

Our orchard is filled with fruit trees, shady picnic spots, fairy paths, and nature nooks. Children gather here to read, snack, swing in hammocks, and rest under apple and pear branches. It’s our favorite place to feel the seasons shift.

The Garden

Our garden is home to raised beds, berry bushes, pollinator plants, and tiny seedlings that grow alongside our Little Farmers. Each child tends their own plot — planting, weeding, watering, and harvesting from spring to fall.

The Mud Kitchen

Mix, stir, splash! In our outdoor mud kitchen and water table, children create imaginative meals with herbs, petals, dirt, pinecones, and water. It’s a fun space for creative play, sensory exploration, and joyful mess-making.

Eagles Next

Another feature of the orchard is our Eagles Nest. Our Little Farms help build and maintain the nest and use this space for more pretend play.

The Nature Weave

Our handmade nature weave is an ongoing project where Little Farmers can weave feathers, grass, flowers, and weeds together as one.
