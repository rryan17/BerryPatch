Meet the Humans

Miss Rachael nourishes with love, laughter, and plenty of silliness. She’s married to Farmer Ian, and together they’re raising a lively little crew of three kids, two dogs, one cat, and eight curious chickens. She’s happiest outside — hands in the soil, surrounded by sunshine, sprouts, and singing birds. Whether she’s planting seeds, gathering eggs, or swaying in the green hammock, Miss Rachael brings a deep love of nature into everything she does.

Beyond her muddy boots and garden gloves, Rachael is also a Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker (LICSW, MSW). She provides supervision for nephrology social workers and brings her knowledge of empathy, child development, and connection to everything she grows — plants and people alike.

Berry Patch Farm School grew from its philosophy of "follow the joy.” This farm school blossomed from Miss Rachael’s deepest passions — children, animals, nature, and the wonder of life. Each element of the Berry Patch was lovingly chosen to spark curiosity, nourish growing hearts, and celebrate the magic found in puddle jumping, buzzing bees, fresh-picked berries, and shared laughter. This is a place where joy leads the way.
