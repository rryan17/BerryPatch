The Orchard

Our orchard is filled with fruit trees, shady picnic spots, fairy paths, and nature nooks. Children gather here to read, snack, swing in hammocks, and rest under apple and pear branches. It’s our favorite place to feel the seasons shift.

The Garden

Our garden is home to raised beds, berry bushes, pollinator plants, and tiny seedlings that grow alongside our Little Farmers. Each child tends their own plot — planting, weeding, watering, and harvesting from spring to fall.

The Mud Kitchen

Mix, stir, splash! In our outdoor mud kitchen, children create imaginative meals with herbs, petals, dirt, and water. It’s a favorite space for creative play, sensory exploration, and joyful mess-making.

The Treehouse

Our handmade treehouse is a quiet hideaway tucked into the trees. Whether pretending it’s a bakery, a birdwatching station, or a pirate ship — it’s a beloved place for imagination and cozy gatherings.
