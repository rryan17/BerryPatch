Welcome!

Welcome to Berry Patch Farm School. We are a nature-rich, in-home program for young children ages 2–5 years. Our days are filled with garden play, animal visits, and seasonal magic.

Located in the heart of Highland Park, Saint Paul, we offer joyful early childhood experiences inspired by the land, seasons, and the belief that young children thrive in nature-based, play-centered environments.
