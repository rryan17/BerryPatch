About Us

Welcome to The Berry Patch, a home-based childcare program rooted in nature, wonder, and connection. We believe in the magic of childhood — muddy boots, curious questions, and caring hearts. Here at the Berry Patch, we center our days around plants, animals, growth, good food, and even better company.

We strive to slow down — to give children the space to take their time, notice the little things, and savor each season. Our projects are often long and layered — from seed to root to harvest, from scrap wood to treehouse. We believe that when kids are given time and trust, they build patience, pride, and deep understanding.

Our philosophy is simple: follow the joy. If something sparks curiosity or lights up a smile — we follow that path. Through playful exploration and meaningful moments on our farm, we aim to nurture a love of the natural world, spark wonder, and foster empathy in every child.

“Let them be little. Let them be wild. Let them be free.”

Growing With Us

Each child will have their own garden bed to tend and will get to choose what to grow. Additionally, each child will grow their favorite snack. For example, if they love sweet popcorn, we’ll plant popping corn in the spring and harvest it in the fall. Come early spring, we’ll tap our maple trees and make syrup to drizzle on top — a snack that grows with us all year long.

Children are also invited to grow a food they don’t like — transforming yuck to yum through care, curiosity, and a little dirt under their fingernails.
