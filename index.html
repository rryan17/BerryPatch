Welcome to Berry Patch Farm School

Welcome to Berry Patch Farm School. We are a nature-rich, in-home program for young children ages 2–5 years. We will be opening soon in Highland Park, Saint Paul. Join us in a joyful space full of imagination, mud, berries, chickens, and childhood wonder.
